  • THE DRESSEL SCHOLARSHIP
    Because so many UW Tacoma students are first-generation students who possibly don’t know the pathways to engagement in their community—or don’t have access to those networks—this scholarship and mentorship program is designed to open doors.
    The financial assistance for Dressel Scholars is designed to free up students’ time so that they can engage in ways that will plant their roots here and weave them into the fabric of this community.

    Our goal is to create a world-class scholars program that is good for students, good for the community, and honors the legacy of leadership that Melanie Dressel embodied.
