  • SCHOLARSHIP INITIATIVES
    We champion publicly engaged scholarship.
    Discovery is at the heart of the university. We value and support a range of scholarly and creative activity broadly defined: Discovery, integration, application, and teaching and learning. Our focus on engaged scholarship bridges the gap between theory and practice. Involving students in the scholarship of the institution enriches their experience and gives our work relevance beyond our disciplines. Publicly engaged scholarship builds on mutually beneficial partnerships and creates positive impacts beyond the campus and academy, connecting the South Sound with the world at large.

  • SUPPORT FACULTY RESEARCH AND TEACHING DEVELOPMENT
    UW Tacoma has a decentralized structure when it comes to development and deliverary of faculty development in research, teaching, leadership, and community engagement. This work group will come up with a plan to review and assess the current stucture and effectiveness of faculty development at UW Tacoma and provide guidence to academic affairs and campus leadership guidence on faculty development.
  • ALIGN P&T GUIDELINES WITH MISSION
    Faculty in each unit will consider (1) the inclusion of community engagement and related scholarly work in their unit-level promotion and tenure guidelines, and (2) overall alignment of promotion and tenure guidelines with the mission and strategic plan of UW Tacoma. (for both lecturer and tenure-track promotions)
  • CREATE RESEARCH ADVISORY COUNCIL
    UW Tacoma lacks clear faculty voice in its shared governance structure. This council is envisioned to play that role. In particular, the council will 1) be a faculty advocate for research and scholarship at the campus, 2) work with the AVC for Research and EVCAA to develop research policies at UW Tacoma 3) provide guidance to Office of Research and Academic Affairs to build infrastructure to support research and scholarship at the campus.
    Two years ago the above mentioned group listed met and prepared the UW Tacoma Research Infrastructure, Support and Compliance document. This new initiative will bring the group back together to 1) review and assess the document for its usefulness, 2) identify the gaps within the UW Tacoma research infrastructure, and 3) produce actionable suggestions to enhance infrastructure and scholarship at UW Tacoma.
  • COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT JOURNALS
    Creation and ongoing maintenance/update of a dabase for all jounrals that publish community engagement/public scholarship articles. These journals will be indexed and the database will be made available to our faculty members who might not be aware of them as an outlet for their publications.
